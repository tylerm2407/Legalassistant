## What This Traction Proves

1. **The problem is real.** 600K+ people watched our content about the gap between lawyer costs and what people can afford. They didn't scroll past — they watched, liked, shared, and followed.

2. **People will pay.** LinkedIn pricing poll: 100% of respondents said they'd pay for CaseMate. 50% validated our exact $20/month price point. 25% would pay more.

3. **The market is underserved.** Legal content on TikTok and Instagram is a blue ocean. There are almost no competitors creating consistent, high-quality legal education content. CaseMate is establishing category leadership early.

4. **Organic growth works.** $0 ad spend → 25,300+ followers and 600K+ views. When we layer paid acquisition on top of this organic base, the unit economics will be exceptional (projected blended CAC < $30 vs $240 LTV).

5. **The content converts.** 85 waitlist signups directly attributed to a single LinkedIn post. Legal tip threads on X drive consistent waitlist traffic. The funnel from content → waitlist → paid subscriber is validated.
