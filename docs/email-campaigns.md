# CaseMate Email Campaigns

Reference doc for Mailchimp setup. Three campaigns to configure before launch.

---

## 1. Welcome Email (Automation)

**Trigger:** New subscriber with "waitlist" tag
**Delay:** Immediate
**From:** CaseMate <hello@casematelaw.com>

**Subject:** You're on the CaseMate waitlist

**Body:**

> Hi there,
>
> Thanks for joining the CaseMate waitlist. You're one step closer to having a legal assistant that actually knows your situation.
>
> **What is CaseMate?**
> CaseMate is a personalized AI legal assistant. It remembers your state, housing situation, employment, and active legal issues — so every answer is specific to you, not generic advice.
>
> The average US lawyer charges $349/hour. CaseMate costs $20/month. That's the gap we're closing.
>
> **What happens next?**
> We'll email you the moment CaseMate is live. Early waitlist members get priority access and launch pricing.
>
> In the meantime, follow us for legal tips and updates:
> - TikTok: @casemate_legal
> - Instagram: @casemate12
> - Facebook: CaseMate Legal
> - X: @casematelaw
> - LinkedIn: CaseMate
>
> — The CaseMate Team

## 2. Feedback Survey (Manual Send)

**When:** 3 days after welcome email
**Audience:** All waitlist subscribers
**From:** CaseMate <hello@casematelaw.com>

**Subject:** Quick question about your legal needs

**Body:**

> Hi there,
>
> We're building CaseMate to help with the legal issues that matter most to you. One quick question:
>
> **What legal issue would you use CaseMate for first?**
>
> - Housing / Landlord-Tenant
> - Employment / Workplace
> - Family Law / Divorce / Custody
> - Consumer Rights / Debt Collection
> - Something else (reply to this email!)
>
> Your answer helps us prioritize what to build first. Just reply — we read every response.
>
> — Tyler, CaseMate Founder

**Note:** Use Mailchimp's survey/poll block or link to a simple Google Form. Replies go to Tyler's inbox.

## Mailchimp Setup Checklist

- [ ] Create audience list (or use existing)
- [ ] Add "waitlist" and "landing_page" tags
- [ ] Set up Welcome Email as automation (trigger: new subscriber with "waitlist" tag)
- [ ] Draft Feedback Survey email as campaign
- [ ] Draft Launch Announcement as campaign (do not send until launch day)
- [ ] Verify reply-to address is monitored
- [ ] Test all emails with preview/test send
